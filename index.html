UberZ

About

This website will help you find riders going in the same direction to have a common meeting point. This way you can call UberX for a cheaper price by effectively splitting the upfront pricing cost by the number of people joining your ride. Similarly for Uber pool you can find another person to share the ride with and be able to approximately split the cost by two. This allows people to save on transportation costs. Get started by creating an account or login for returning users.
